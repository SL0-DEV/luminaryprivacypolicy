Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2026

Welcome to Luminary. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Luminary is designed to work without collecting personal data from you. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information for advertising or marketing purposes.

  • We do not collect account information: You can use the app without creating an account.
  • We do not collect personal content: We do not read, store, or sell your messages, files, photos, or contacts.
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising: We do not use device information to track you across apps or websites.

2. App Data Stored on Your Device

Any information you enter into Luminary, such as app preferences or in-app content, is stored locally on your device unless stated otherwise. If you delete the app, this data may be removed from your device.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not use your data for advertising, profiling, resale, or cross-app tracking.

  • To provide and maintain app functionality.
  • To save your settings and preferences on your device.
  • To improve app stability and performance in a privacy-friendly way.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. If the app uses any third-party services in the future, this policy will be updated to explain what is shared and why.

At this time, Luminary does not use third-party advertising networks or data brokers.

5. Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

6. Children’s Privacy

Luminary is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

  • You may stop using the app at any time.
  • You may clear app data through your device settings, depending on your platform.
  • You may contact us with privacy questions or deletion requests if any support data was ever provided voluntarily.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information handled by the app, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@luminary.app
