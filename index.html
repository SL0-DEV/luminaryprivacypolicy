Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

Welcome to Luminary. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better experience for all our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you sign in via Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • App Data: We store your habits, tasks, completion history, and hydration logs to provide the core tracking features of the app.
  • AI Interactions: Messages exchanged with the AI Coach are stored to provide context and continuity for your coaching sessions.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic information about your device to optimize the app's performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve Luminary.
  • To personalize your AI coaching experience.
  • To track your progress and award badges/achievements.
  • To process virtual currency transactions in the Store.
  • To send you reminders and notifications (if enabled).

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. AI and Data Processing

Luminary uses the Gemini AI API to power the AI Coach. When you interact with the coach, your messages and relevant app data (like your task list) are processed by the AI to provide helpful responses. We do not use your personal data to train public AI models.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and database storage.
  • Google Gemini API: For AI coaching and analysis.
  • DiceBear: For generating avatar placeholders.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time. You can manage your profile settings within the app or contact us to request data deletion.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@luminary.app
